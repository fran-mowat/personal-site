About

Hello, I'm Fran! I am a second-year computer science student at the University of Westminster. I have a strong interest in technology, particularly in software development, machine learning, and cyber security.

My curiosity and passion for learning have driven me to explore many opportunities within these fields, exemplified by my recent competition of the AI Programming with Python Nanodegree, where I trained a deep-learning model to classify flower images into over 100 distinct species.

Moreover, I have recently completed a 4-month technology internship at an EV charging company. During this period, I delivered various projects to streamline business processes and drive productivity. For instance, I built a Flask web application to redirect 1000+ QR codes to the correct URLs by pulling in charge point data from an API.
