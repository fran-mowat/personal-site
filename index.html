About

Hello, I'm Fran! I am a third-year Computer Science student currently undertaking a technology industrial placement at Morgan Stanley in the Investment Banking and Global Capital Markets Technology team.

With a strong interest in computer science, particularly software development, machine learning, and cyber security, I constantly pursue opportunities to expand my technical skills. For instance, I recently completed an AI Programming with Python Nanodegree, where I trained a deep learning model to classify images of flowers into over 100 distinct species.

Moreover, I interned at an EV charging company across two placements, totalling 6 months, where I delivered technology projects to streamline business processes and drive productivity. For example, I developed a Flask web application to manage 1000+ QR code redirects using charge point data from an API. I also automated competitor utilisation and downtime analysis across 17,000+ UK locations using AWS Lambda, storing the data in a PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS RDS.
