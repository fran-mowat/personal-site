About

Hello, I'm Fran! I am a Computer Science student at the University of Westminster (UOW). I have a strong interest in technology, particularly within the fields of machine learning, cyber security and cloud computing.

My love of learning and curious nature have driven me to explore many opportunities within these fields, exemplified by my recent competition of the Introducing Generative AI with AWS course, involving a project to fine-tune a large language model using Amazon SageMaker.

Alongside my studies, I am part of the UOW AWS DeepRacer Team, where I am developing a reward function to train an autonomous vehicle via reinforcement learning, and am an active member of the Google Developer Student Club (GDSC).
